About Me

Who am I?

Evelyn Aranas

I am a clinical psychologist interested in providing quality research based therapeutic interventions across all aspects of psychological functioning, including cognitive, emotional, behavioural, spiritual, and intellectual dimensions. As a clinical psychologist, I assess, advise, and assist people to manage issues like depression, anxiety, adjustment, trauma, cognition and behaviour. I help clients to achieve and maximise function, participation, and wellbeing in their everyday life.

All psychological therapy is a two-way process and research has shown that the outcome of psychological therapy is improved when the therapist and client agree early about what the major problems are and how psychological therapy can help.

Psychological therapy works best when you attend all scheduled sessions and give some thinking ahead of time as to what you want to discuss during each session. Therapy sessions usually last for about an hour. The length of time in therapy depends on each person. You may meet for only a short time or in some cases, for several months. Before we begin, I will discuss with you the likely length of the therapy.
